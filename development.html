Concept

Colony 12 started with the idea of creating an immersive, interactive movie. While the Oculus Rift was primarily made for the gaming industry, we thought it would be a great tool for creating an immersive experience. This affordable piece of virtual reality technology, paired with exceptional sound quality, allowed us to make Colony 12 come alive.

Technologies Used

Colony 12 brings together the power and flexibility of the Unreal Engine and the immersiveness of the Oculus Rift. Bringing these two technologies together has created an experience that allows for interaction not possible by traditional real-time experiences.
