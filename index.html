Experience another world

Colony 12 is an interactive machinima that uses the power of the Oculus Rift to offer a fully immersive stereoscopic storytelling experience. Strap on the headset and prepare to be taken on a journey through the destroyed alien world as you learn more about the crash that led you there and the dangers of your new surroundings.

Two Humanities. One War.

As humanity expanded it moved many of it's cililization to large space fleets that traveled the vastness of interstellar space to seek out and colonize uninhabited planets. Those who left the fleet to join the colonies below were shaped by their surroundings through rapid evolution splitting humanity into two key factions; the United Colonies of Earth and the Biotics, known more simply as just the BIOs.

Step into the Rift.

Colony 12 uses the power of virtual reality to completely immerse its users into an alien world. Using the Oculus Rift's directional tracking and fully immersive technology, users can truely feel what it is like to be stranded on an unknown world not knowing what lays in store for them on the journey ahead.
