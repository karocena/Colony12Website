Node-Based Story

Colony 12 uses a node-based plot system to guide the user through the experience, while noting what the user looks at throughout the experience. We use this information to generate custom sequences influenced by the details that a user notices along their journey.

This level of implicit interation adds a new layer to the media form and enriches the experience all while the user remains completely unaware until they decide to experience the world again.

Two humanities. One war.

Humanity, no matter how evolved, can never escape the grips of human nature and the constant quest for superiority.

Following a sudden cataclysmic event on Earth that put the survival of the entire human race at risk, the world governments united to form what is known as UCE: the United Colonies of Earth. This coalition unanimously agreed to colonize space; marking the beginning of a new era.

Humans began establishing settlements on new planets while others chose to remain in space. Those who ventured to new planets used genetic catalysts to help adapt to their new worlds, altering their physical abilities and appearances. These new variants of humanity became known as the BIOs. Threatened by the rapid evolution of the BIOs, the space colonies began experimenting with cybernetics to enhance their natural abilities. Over the next century, following numerous failed attempts to assert control over the BIOs through diplomacy, tensions reached a tipping point; plunging both races into war.

The first wave of UCE forces were met with heavy resistance from the BIOs. Few made it to the and those who did were not prepared for what waiting for them below...
